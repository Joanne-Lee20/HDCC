CREATIVE WORKSHOP PROJECTS

For six weeks, the classroom became a Creative Workshop, aiming to explore what can be created with generative AI tools. After podcast interviews, students refined and improved the content. Embracing an experimental approach, they used generative AI tools to create texts, images, music, and videos contributing to discussions on generative AI in both campus and public settings. Small groups proposed project plans, and students worked on two significant projects organized in "waves." The Creative Workshop's assessment involved a public work log, where students reported their daily activities and self-evaluated using a 5-point scale through a designated form.
